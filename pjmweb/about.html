Since 2000

P.J. Metal House (2000) Co. Ltd., located in Samut Prakan, Thailand, was established on January 1, 1999.

We have over 20 years of expertise and experience in forming and manufacturing high-quality steel and stainless steel that reliable for a long time.

We also sell steel and stainless steel sheets, both wholesale and retail, provide metal sheet services according to the design to serve the needs of customers in a variety of markets such as cutting services by various technologies, including laser, scissors, plasma, water jet, v-cut / folding services , rolling, welding and assembly, laser marking, punching, etc.

We are committed to bringing the latest technology from abroad to raise the standard of products and services, increase business value for entrepreneurs in Thailand and in the ASEAN countries.
